Hi, there! Welcome to my website. I am a Master’s student in Robotics, Cognition, and Intelligence at the Technical University of Munich. During my master study, I am passionate about computer vision, robotics perception, and machine learning, with a focus on 3D reconstruction, SLAM, and vision-based robotic systems. I have experience working on multi-view stereo, LiDAR-vision fusion, and learning-based methods for robust perception in real-world environments.

Currently, I am particularly interested in:
- Computer vision for robotics
- 3D reconstruction and scene understanding
- Multi-sensor perception
- Learning-based methods for robust robotic systems

Please feel free to explore my website to know more about my projects and personal life!
